Consent Form

This keystroke experiment is being produced as part of the coursework for Computer Science course CS229 at Stanford University. Participants in experimental evaluation of this analysis provide their keystroke identity used to analyze in further individual classification. Data will be collected by the timestamp and characters/symbols that participant responds on their keyboard.

Participation in this experiment is voluntary. Participants may withdraw themselves and their data at any time without fear of consequences. Concerns about the experiment may be discussed with the researcher Paphop Sawasdee. Participant anonymity will be provided by the separate storage of names from data. Data will only be identified by participant number. No identifying information about the participants will be available to anyone except the researcher.

I hereby acknowledge that I have been given an opportunity to ask questions about the nature of the experiment and my participation in it. I give my consent to have data collected on my behavior and opinions in relation to the experiment. I also give permission for images/video while using the application to be used in presentations or publications as long as I am not personally identifiable in the images/video. I understand I may withdraw my permission at any time.
